Book John Thompson

"I Can. I Will." isn't just a speech—it's a challenge to every person in the room.

John Thompson doesn't step onto the stage to talk about what he lost. He speaks about what he found.

After losing his leg, facing chronic pain, addiction, depression, financial hardship, and countless setbacks, John refused to let one word define his future:

Can't.

Through grit, faith, determination, and an unwavering belief that every setback can become a comeback, John rebuilt his life and founded Lost Limb Riders—a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping amputees and their families reclaim freedom, confidence, independence, and purpose.

John's keynote is emotional, humorous, brutally honest, and deeply inspiring. Audiences don't simply hear his story—they see themselves in it. They leave believing they can overcome disability, trauma, addiction, grief, mental health struggles, career setbacks, and life's toughest obstacles.

What Your Audience Will Learn

  • Replace excuses with action.
  • Build resilience one decision at a time.
  • Turn adversity into opportunity.
  • Develop purpose that is stronger than fear.
  • Why "I Can. I Will." is more than a slogan—it's a mindset.
  • How ordinary people accomplish extraordinary things by refusing to quit.
