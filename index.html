Booking John Thompson:

"I Can. I Will." isn't just a speech—it's a challenge to every person in the room.After losing his leg, facing unimaginable setbacks, chronic pain, addiction, depression, and countless obstacles, John refused to let one word define his future: Can't. Through grit, faith, determination, and an unwavering belief that every setback can become a comeback, John rebuilt his life—and founded Lost Limb Riders, an organization dedicated to helping others reclaim their freedom, confidence, and purpose. John's keynote is emotional, humorous, brutally honest, and deeply inspiring. Audiences don't just hear his story—they see themselves in it. They leave believing they can overcome the challenges standing in front of them, whether those challenges involve disability, trauma, addiction, grief, mental health, leadership, or simply life's unexpected hardships.
